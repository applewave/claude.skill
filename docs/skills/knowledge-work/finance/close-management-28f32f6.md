## 주요 내용

- **진행 방식**: Common Bottlenecks and Solutions Close Retrospective Questions After each close, ask: What went well this close that we should continue? What took longer than expected and why? What blockers did we encounter and how can we prevent them? Were there any surprises in the financial results we should have caught earlier? What can we automate or streamline for next month?
