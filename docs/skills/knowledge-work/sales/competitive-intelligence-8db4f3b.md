## 주요 내용

- **산출물**: The skill generates a **self-contained HTML file** with: 1. Comparison Matrix (Landing View) Overview comparing you vs. all competitors at a glance: Feature comparison grid Pricing comparison Market positioning Win rate indicators (if CRM connected) 2. Competitor Tabs (Click to Expand) Each competitor gets a clickable card that expands to show: Company profile (size, funding, target market) What they sell and how they position Recent releases (last 90 days) Where they win vs. where you win Pricing intelligence Talk tracks for different scenarios Objection handling Landmine questions 3. Your Company Card Your releases (last 90 days) Your key differentiators Proof points and customer quotes
